Pinkball

2D platformer where your goal is to eat all the junk food.

Made me unconstrain myself by creating scripts, 2D sprites and sounds from scratch.

  • Added an audio manager to handle sound and music playback.
  • Used Aseprite in creating sprite sheets.
  • Created background music in Ableton and cleaned up samples in Audacity.
